Dining

NYU Abu Dhabi provides a diverse range of food choices to ensure that the community stays energized and well-fed trought the day. There are numerous dining options available in and around the NYUAD campus. At the marketplace, you can find a wide selection of cuisines such as Indian, Mexican, and Italian, along with various desserts options.

The popular dining halls, Campus East and Campus West, offer a favorite gathering spot for the community, featuring rotating menus and different themed counters serving break lunch, and dinner. For those seeking a tranquil study environment, the Library Cafe is the perfect choice, where you can enjoy specialty tea, fresh coffee, and delectable homemade pastries

Promoting Sustainable Dining Practices

To promote sustainability in our dining practices, NYUAD encourages the use of refillable water bottles instead of single-use plastic bottles on campus. Water stations have been strategically placed throughout the campus to ensure convenient access to drinking water while also minimizing our reliance on plastic.
