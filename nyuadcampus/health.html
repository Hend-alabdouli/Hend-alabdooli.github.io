Activities and Wellbeing

During your four-year journey at NYUAD, you can expect to invest significant time in attending classes, studying at the library and completing internships. Despitethe demanding nature of these commitments, it is crucial to prioritize relaxation and cherish moments spent with friends. Fortunately, NYUAD campus offers a wide array of activities for you to explore and enjoy.


The NYU Abu Dhabi Fitness Center is an advanced facility situated within the Campus Center building. It boasts cutting-edge equimpment for cardiovascular workouts, resistance training, and free weight exercises, ensuring a comprehensive fitness experience.

The open recreational area in the Campus Center, known as Baraha, is bustling with activity. Baraha offers a diverse range of gaming amenities, including air hockey, a pool table, a pinball machine, and various other entertainment options.

The film screening room is equipped with a projector and an integrated high-definition sound and projection system, providing a cinematic ambiance that is perfect for hosting private screenings.

NYUAD offers comfortable spaces for reading, dedicated areas for computer use, and study rooms designed for both small and large groups. These amenities are provide to cultivate a productive research atmosphere.
